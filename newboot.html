About Me

What I'd Like You To Know:

I was the jack of all trades, the account manager, tester, mockup maker, dream weaver, Google analyzer, SEO optimizer, client liason, trainer, documentation maker, graphics designer, E-commerce builder, Joomla, Wordpress and Typo3 content manage and frontend developer.
